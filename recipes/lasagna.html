Lasagna

Home

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is the ultimate comfort food with layers of rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and gooey cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the beef with onion and garlic.
  2. Add tomato sauce and simmer.
  3. Boil the noodles.
  4. Layer sauce, noodles, and cheeses.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes.
