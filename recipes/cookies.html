Chocolate Chip Cookies

Home

Description

These chewy, gooey chocolate chip cookies are a favorite for all ages.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Mix dry and wet ingredients.
  2. Add chocolate chips.
  3. Scoop onto tray.
  4. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 10-12 minutes.
